Total catch = sum-over-age-classes(catch-at-age * weight-in-the-catch-at-age)
SSB = sum-over-age-classes(numbers-at-age * weight-in-the-stock-at-age * maturity-at-age)

File names:
- Catch.txt = total catch (in tonnes)
- Catch_AY.txt = catch at age (in numbers)
- FatAge.txt = fishing mortality at age (for the "average" fishing mortality look up the age classes contributing to it. They vary from stock to stock)
- MatAge.txt = natural mortality at age (less relevant)
- NatAge.txt = numbers at age ("cohort size")
- PatAge.txt = maturity at age
- R_SSB.txt = recruitment (less relevant) and SSB
- WatAge.txt = weight-in-the-stock at age
- WatAge_Catch.txt = weight-in-the-catch at age
